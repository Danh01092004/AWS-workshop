**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? *What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [AWS VPC, EC2, Networking Fundamentals & Route 53 DNS Setup](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [VPC, EC2, Storage Management & Hands-on Networking Practice](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Amazon S3 Static Website Hosting & Amazon RDS Database Setup](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [WordPress Deployment, CloudFront CDN & AWS Managed AD Integration](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Serverless Architecture: Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB & CloudFormation](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Container Technology: Docker, ECR, ECS Fargate & CI/CD Pipeline](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Advanced Networking: VPC Peering, Transit Gateway, Direct Connect & Hybrid Cloud](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [CI/CD Automation: CodePipeline, CodeBuild, CodeDeploy & Safe Deployments](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [VM Migration with VMWare, S3 Management, Storage Gateway & CloudFront](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [AWS Security: IAM, Cognito, Security Hub, KMS & Resource Tagging](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Data Services: S3, Glue, Athena, DynamoDB, QuickSight & Data Analytics](1.12-week12/)
